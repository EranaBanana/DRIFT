🧠 How to Use Drift
🎯 The One Thing
Every day, pick one task that matters most. This banner is your anchor — if everything else falls apart, doing this one thing counts as a win. Click the teal banner and type it in.
✦ Click the checkbox on the banner to mark it complete and get a confetti reward!
📋 Tasks & Timer
Add tasks using the + Add Task button or quick-add at the bottom. Every task has a priority and time estimate.
▶ Click the ▶ play button on any task to instantly link it to the timer.
▶ The timer will auto-set the duration from the task's estimate.
▶ Drag & drop a task onto the timer circle to lock in and start focusing.
▶ When the timer ends, you'll be asked if the task is done.
⏱ Hyperfocus Guard Timer
The timer on the right is your Pomodoro + hyperfocus guard. Set it to 25 minutes (or your preferred focus sprint). Enable Hyperfocus guard to get a nudge every 45 mins to eat, drink water, and move.
🌀 Brain Dump
Feeling overwhelmed? Open Brain Dump and write everything flooding your brain — no structure, no rules. Then use the prefix buttons to tag lines:
→ Action item    • Thought    ⚠ Worry    ? Question    💡 Idea
Click "Extract tasks" to pull all → action lines into a list you can promote to Today.
🅿️ Parking Lot Features
💭 Distraction Capture — when your brain wanders mid-task, park the thought here instead of switching tasks.

🅿️ Not Now — tasks that matter but not today. Your brain can let go once it's parked.

🛑 Impulse Pause — want to buy something? Park it for 48 hours. See if you still want it then.
💾 Keeping Your Data Safe
Drift saves everything to your browser's local storage — nothing goes to a server. Export a backup regularly using ↓ Export in the sidebar. The backup pill shows your last export status.
⚠ Clearing browser data will erase your planner — always keep a recent export!
